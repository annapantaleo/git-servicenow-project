  1. Go to Palo del Colle train station
  2. Buy a ticket for Bari
  3. Take the train and wait for your stop
  4. Once in Bari Centrale get off the train and go to Via Garruba
  5. In Via Garruba you will notice a big building called DIPARTIMENTO DI LINGUE E LETTERATURE STRANIERE
